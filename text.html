Yhwach

Yhwach The Almighty

Yhwach (ユーハバッハ, Yūhabahha), called "Your Majesty" (陛下, Heika) by his underlings, is the emperor of the Wandenreich and the progenitor of Quincy, known as the Quincy King (滅却師クインシーの王, Kuinshī no Ō) and Father of Quincy (滅却師クインシーの父, Kuinshī no Chichi).[3][4][5] He is also the son of the Soul King[6] and inherited the latter's signature power, The Almighty,[2] signified by Yhwach bearing the letter A.[1]
